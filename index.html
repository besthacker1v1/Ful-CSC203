All Topics to Focus On

This 200-level Computer Science course is quite demanding and will require your best effort. Below are the main topics to master:

  • Logic and Proofs

  • Algorithms and Complexity
  • Combinatorics

Goals

Key objectives to strengthen your understanding of CSC 203:

  1. Logic and Proofs: Master truth tables and write 20 proofs.
  2. Sets, Functions, and Relations: Solve 50 problems (Venn, injectivity, etc.).
  3. Counting and Graphs: Complete 10 graph problems and 20 combinatorics exercises.
  4. Induction, Recurrence, and Review: Solve 5 induction proofs and 3 recurrences.
